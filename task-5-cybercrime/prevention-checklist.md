* Verify URLs: Always check for "https://" and ensure the domain name is spelled correctly (e.g., vitbhopal.ac.in).
* Enable MFA: Use Multi-Factor Authentication on all email and social media accounts.
* UPI Safety: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; a PIN is only required for sending.
* Secure QR Codes: Do not scan random QR codes sent via WhatsApp or stuck in public places.
* Software Updates: Keep your OS and antivirus software updated to patch security vulnerabilities.
* Public Wi-Fi: Avoid logging into bank accounts or sensitive portals while on unprotected public Wi-Fi.
* Strong Passwords: Use a mix of symbols, numbers, and cases, and never reuse the same password.
* Official Reporting: If targeted, report immediately to the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the 1930 Helpline.
