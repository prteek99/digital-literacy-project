Social Media Do's for College Students



* Maintain a Professional Profile: Use a clean profile picture and a bio that reflects your academic interests as a student at VIT Bhopal.
* Verify Before Sharing: Always check the facts and sources of a post before sharing it to avoid spreading misinformation.
* Use Privacy Settings: Regularly review your account security and privacy settings to control who can see your personal information.
* Network with Industry Leaders: Use platforms like LinkedIn to follow and engage with professionals in the AI and tech fields.
* Think Before You Post: Ensure your content is respectful and adds value to your digital footprint.



Social Media Don'ts for College Students



* Don't Post Offensive Content: Avoid sharing any material that could be seen as biased, disrespectful, or unprofessional.
* Don't Share Private Location Details: Never post your live location or sensitive personal data like your home address or phone number.
* Don't Engage with Trolls: Avoid public online arguments or "flame wars" that can damage your reputation.
* Don't Tag Others Without Permission: Always respect the privacy of your peers before posting photos or videos of them.
* Don't Post Confidential Info: Never share internal college documents, exam papers, or private communication publicly.
